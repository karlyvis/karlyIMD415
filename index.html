I grew up on a dairy farm outside Thunder Bay for my whole life. I am involved in many sports but my main focus was always hockey. As I grew up I knew that I would want a hands on job. There I played the best year of hockey in my life along with taking art classes to improved my drawing and design skills. But after a year I decided that living away from home was not something for me.

So now I am a student at Confederation College in Thunder Bay taking the Interactive Media Design course and I have never been so excited. I love this course and everything I have learnt so far.

I hope to work for an amazing employer and be apart of a team that will help me grow not only as a designer but also as a person
